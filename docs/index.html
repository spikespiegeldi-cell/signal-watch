Indicator Reference Guide (click to expand)
Tech Chain
arXiv papers 7-day moving average of academic papers submitted to arXiv matching your tracked keywords (total past 7 days ÷ 7, expressed as papers/day). Using a weekly average smooths out weekend quiet periods and batch submission patterns. A rising average suggests a research wave is forming before it reaches mainstream media.
Hacker News Total story count on HN matching your keywords. High counts indicate the developer/tech community is actively discussing the topic — often an early adoption signal.
USPTO Patents 7-day moving average of patent applications filed by your tracked companies (total past 7 days ÷ 7, expressed as filings/day). USPTO data has processing lags so a weekly window captures filings more reliably than a single day. A rising average often precedes a product launch or signals a company racing to protect new IP.
Crunchbase Count of funding news items (raises, seed rounds, Series A) matching tech/AI keywords in the past 24h. Rising counts indicate investor capital is concentrating in a sector.
Economy Chain
Yield Curve (T10Y2Y) The difference between the 10-year and 2-year US Treasury yields, in percentage points. Positive = normal economy. Negative (inverted) = historically predicts recession within 12–18 months. Source: US Federal Reserve (FRED).
Box Production US cardboard box production index from the Federal Reserve (FRED series IPG2653S). Boxes are used to ship physical goods — a drop in production is an early warning of slowing consumer demand and supply chain contraction.
Marine Traffic Count of recent news articles mentioning Shanghai port congestion. Port backlogs are a leading indicator of global trade disruption — spikes often precede supply chain stress and commodity price moves.
Jobs Proxy Job listing count via Indeed RSS for tracked roles. A sudden drop signals hiring freezes and corporate caution; a surge signals expansion. Used as a proxy for LinkedIn job market activity.
Biotech Chain
NIH Reporter Total active US government research grants this fiscal year matching your keywords (e.g. protein folding, CAR-T). A high and rising grant count means federal money is flowing into a drug category — a multi-year leading indicator of clinical activity.
bioRxiv 7-day moving average of biology preprints submitted to bioRxiv (total past 7 days ÷ 7, expressed as preprints/day). Preprints are published before peer review — the earliest public signal of research momentum. The weekly average removes day-of-week submission noise.
ClinicalTrials.gov Number of clinical trials started in the past 30 days matching your keywords. A surge indicates pharma companies are moving from lab to human testing — typically 3–5 years ahead of market approval.
SEC S-1 Filings Biotech company IPO registration filings with the SEC. Companies file S-1s when preparing to go public. A cluster of biotech S-1s signals that investors see a window for a specific drug category.
Social Chain
Bluesky Posts Total posts on Bluesky mentioning your tracked keywords (autonomous vehicle, drug discovery, protein folding, etc.) in the past 24h. Bluesky skews toward researchers, journalists, and tech professionals — a spike here often reflects expert community discussion before topics reach mainstream media.
Google Trends Average search interest score (0–100) for your tracked terms over the past 7 days. 100 = peak popularity. A rising score means more people are searching for the topic — a direct measure of mainstream curiosity.
Kickstarter Number of new crowdfunding projects launched in the past 24h. Consumer crowdfunding is a leading indicator of fringe ideas going mainstream — products often appear on Kickstarter 1–2 years before retail.
Amazon Movers Number of items appearing in Amazon's Movers & Shakers list (biggest sales rank gainers). Sudden spikes in a product category indicate a consumer behaviour shift that is already happening at scale.
Geopolitics Chain
Commodity Prices Percentage of tracked commodities (lithium, copper, wheat, crude oil, gold) that are trading above their own 30-day average today. 100 = all rising, 0 = all falling, ~50 = normal mixed market. Each commodity is compared to its own baseline so different price units (USD/barrel vs USD/oz) do not cancel each other out. A reading above 60 suggests broad commodity strength — often a leading signal of inflation, supply stress, or geopolitical tension.
Shipping Congestion News article count mentioning Shanghai port disruption in the past 7 days. Shanghai handles ~20% of global container traffic — congestion here is an early warning of global supply chain stress.
EU Consultations Count of currently open European Commission consultations. A spike in a policy area (e.g. AI regulation, energy) signals upcoming legislation that could reshape industries across all EU member states 1–3 years ahead.
Congress Hearings Number of US congressional committee hearings in the past 7 days. Hearing clusters on a specific topic (e.g. AI, pharma pricing) are an early signal of coming US regulation or government action.
Corporate Chain
SEC Form 4 Insider buying/selling filings by executives and directors of your tracked companies. Executives buying their own stock in size is historically one of the strongest signals of upcoming positive news — they know their company better than anyone.
Jobs Proxy Job listing count for your tracked companies. Sudden hiring sprees in a specific function (e.g. a pharma company hiring 50 manufacturing engineers) can signal a product launch or major expansion weeks before any announcement.
Patents Patent filings by your watchlist companies. Companies file patents 12–18 months before products launch. A cluster of filings in a new technology area signals a strategic pivot or acquisition target.
SEC 8-K Filings Material event disclosures filed by your watchlist companies. 8-Ks cover major events: acquisitions, executive departures, new contracts, legal actions. A surge signals significant corporate activity across your portfolio.
Energy Chain
ARPA-E Grants US Advanced Research Projects Agency – Energy press releases in the past 30 days. ARPA-E funds high-risk, high-reward energy research. A wave of announcements in a technology area (e.g. SMR, fusion, grid storage) signals a US government technology bet 5–10 years before commercialisation.
arXiv Physics Applied physics preprints (category: physics.app-ph) submitted in the past 24h. Covers energy storage, nuclear, photovoltaics, superconductors. A spike indicates a research breakthrough or concentrated academic activity in a technology area.
Energy Commodities Average spot price (USD) of lithium, uranium, and copper — the three key materials for energy transition. Lithium = battery storage, Uranium = nuclear renaissance, Copper = electrification infrastructure. Price moves here lead investment waves by 12–24 months.
Crunchbase Energy Energy-sector funding news items (battery, solar, nuclear, EV, grid) in the past 24h from Crunchbase. Venture capital flowing into a specific energy technology is a 3–7 year leading indicator of commercial deployment.
